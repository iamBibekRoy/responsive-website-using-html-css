About us

It was the summer of 2009 when the doors first opened in the heart of New York City. The owners, two childhood friends, had a dream of creating a space where people could come together, relax, and enjoy.

The Pub & Lounge we became a staple in the neighbourhood, with its cozy atmosphere, friendly staff, and extensive menu of drinks and food. From local craft beers to classic cocktails, the pub had something for everyone.

Over the years, The Pub & Lounge continued to thrive and grow, hosting events and gatherings that brought the community together. From trivia nights to live music performances, the pub was always buzzing with activity.

Now, more than a decade later, The Pub & Lounge continues to serve up great drinks, delicious food, and unforgettable memories for locals and visitors alike. And as the city around it continues to change, it remains a constant, a place where people can always come back to for a taste of that New York City spirit.
